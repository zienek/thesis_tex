Sound localisation is a complex problem related to many matters of signal processing.
This Master Thesis contains few most common methodology in sound localization.
Main focus is taken into comparing those. Signal processing approach in sound localisation
needs to clarify such matters as:

\begin{itemize}
\item {Analog signal processing}
\item {Digital signal processing}
\item {Analog to Digital Conversion}
\item {Time difference of arrival}
\item {Cross-Correlation with few modifications:
	\begin{itemize}
	\item {GCC Generalized Cross-Correlation}
	\item {PHAT Phase Transform}
	\item {ML Maximum Likelihood}
	\end{itemize}
}
\item {CORDIC equations}

\end{itemize}
